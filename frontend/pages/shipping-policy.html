Shipping Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Shipping Areas

We currently ship to all major cities and towns across India. International shipping is not available at this time.

2. Processing Time

  • Standard Orders: 1-2 business days for processing
  • Custom Orders: 3-5 business days for processing
  • Orders placed after 2 PM IST will be processed the next business day
  • No processing on Sundays and national holidays

3. Delivery Time

Standard Shipping

5-7 business days

₹200 (Free on orders above ₹10,000)

Express Shipping

2-3 business days

₹500

4. Shipping Partners

We partner with trusted courier services:

  • Delhivery
  • DTDC
  • Blue Dart
  • India Post

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive:

  • Shipping confirmation email with tracking number
  • Regular updates on your order status
  • Estimated delivery date
  • Courier contact information

6. Shipping Charges

  • Free Shipping: On all orders above ₹10,000
  • Standard Shipping: ₹200 for orders below ₹10,000
  • Express Shipping: ₹500 (available at checkout)
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable

7. Delivery Issues

In case of delivery issues:

  • Failed delivery attempts - 3 attempts will be made
  • Wrong address provided - customer bears return shipping
  • Refused delivery - restocking fee may apply
  • Contact us within 24 hours of delivery issues
